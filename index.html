Featured Project: Amazon Rainforest Conservation

VCS Verified

Protecting 1,000 hectares of pristine Amazon rainforest, this project sequesters 5,000 tons of CO₂ annually while supporting indigenous communities and preserving critical biodiversity.

The Amazon Rainforest, often called the "lungs of the Earth," is home to 10% of the world's biodiversity and plays a crucial role in global climate regulation. Our conservation project protects 1,000 hectares in Brazil's Amazonas state, preventing deforestation while promoting sustainable land use practices and empowering local communities.
