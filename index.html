Save the Amazon Rainforest

Protect 1000 hectares of vital rainforest, sequester 5000 tons CO2 yearly, and support indigenous communities.

The Amazon Rainforest, Earth’s largest tropical forest, absorbs vast CO2, hosts 10% of global biodiversity, and sustains 400 indigenous communities. Our project preserves 1000 hectares in Brazil’s Amazonas state, preventing deforestation, promoting sustainable land use, and empowering local stewards.
